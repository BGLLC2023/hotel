# Super 8 Hotel Analytics

## Overview

This project analyzes Super 8 hotel performance data from 2023–2026 to identify optimal pricing strategies for April. By examining historical RevPAR (revenue per available room), occupancy rates, and average daily rate (ADR) trends, this analysis provides data-driven recommendations to maximize revenue during peak demand periods.

## Project Goal

The objective was to identify optimal pricing strategies for April that maximize RevPAR while maintaining competitive occupancy rates. The analysis establishes a performance baseline to determine which years performed best and inform future pricing decisions.

## Key Findings

### 1. **April 2023 as the Performance Benchmark**
- April 2023 recorded the highest average revenue across all weekdays
- Demonstrates the strongest combination of peak height and sustained duration
- Provides the optimal model for pricing strategy going forward

### 2. **Mid-to-Late April Demand Spike Pattern**
- A consistent spike in RevPAR emerges from mid-to-late April across all three years (2023–2025)
- The spike is accompanied by corresponding increases in both revenue and occupancy
- Pattern suggests predictable seasonal demand that can be leveraged for pricing optimization

### 3. **Year-over-Year Comparative Analysis**
- **2023**: Best overall performance (highest peak + sustained duration)
- **2024**: Lowest peak with comparable duration to 2025
- **2025**: Highest single peak but shortest sustained duration
- Clear recovery trend in 2025 suggests market stabilization

### 4. **Early-Month Demand Signal**
- RevPAR spike in April 2023 began growing around **April 10th**
- This inflection point indicates strong early-month demand
- Represents optimal timing for price increases

## Recommendations

### 1. **Adopt 2023 ADR Strategy**
Implement the April 2023 pricing model, which demonstrated:
- Highest occupancy rates across all analyzed years
- Best balance of ADR and RevPAR performance
- Sustainable revenue generation throughout the month

### 2. **Capitalize on Early-Month Demand (April 10th+)**
The RevPAR spike in April 2023 beginning around April 10th indicates strong seasonal demand:
- **Action**: Increase ADR starting April 10th to align with demand patterns
- **Expected Impact**: Incremental revenue growth without sacrificing occupancy
- **Timing**: Implement price increases before peak demand fully materializes

### 3. **Implement Tiered Pricing Strategy**
- **Pre-Peak (April 1–9)**: Maintain competitive baseline rates to capture early bookings
- **Peak Period (April 10–30)**: Apply 2023 ADR strategy to maximize revenue during high-demand period
- **Post-Peak (May onwards)**: Adjust rates based on occupancy and demand signals

### 4. **Monitor and Adjust**
- Track occupancy and RevPAR metrics weekly during April
- Compare performance against 2023 baseline
- Be prepared to adjust pricing if occupancy drops below historical targets
